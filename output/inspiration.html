Presentazioni

A small program to create attractive presentations starting from a markdown. I believe I need it because I have the impression that it will be necessary during my career and life to create different presentations. So I need something that allows me to create a nice presentation from a template quickly and easily.

Presentations are managed in markdown. In fact, the inspiration for this program comes from Obsidian's slides. On the other hand, I noticed that one of my professors used very simple and nice slides, which moved using the same Vim keybindings. It seems to me that the program used by my professor to manage web pages was written in Java. I haven't investigated much about the latter, I just got inspired by the graphical interface. The idea is to combine these two things (it seems to me that Obsidian slides also have Vim keybindings, but that's not the only thing I took from my teacher's slides).
